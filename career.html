The Beginning.

Currently I am the General Manager of Collaboration Software at LEAP Dev.
Prior to this role in 2019 I was the Chief Executive Officer of Zaliet, a company created by LEAP to help connect clients and lawyers. I was reporting directly to the Executive Chairman and was a member of LEAP’s Executive Team, however with my recent choice to shift careers I am now reporting to the CEO of LEAPDev who manages over 100 developers that build and maintain a suite of software products globally.

A big part of this transition, was to delegate all client facing roles such as Sales, Marketing, Installation and Support to the local countries to enable me to focus purely on Software whilst i study.

The Middle.

Since joining LEAP in 2010, I have moved through the company originally being employed on the HelpDesk taking calls through to teh top. I have been in the following roles:
State Implementation Manager Quality Assurance Manager National Implementation Manager
Global Data Migration Manager CEO of Zaliet
General Manager of Collaboration Software

Over the years, my team and I have pioneered a new way for law firms to get paid faster, manage online bookings and collaborate with their clients through Zaliet’s online Web portals. We continue to focus on building world-class software for law firms globally.

Not the End.

After 13 years in Legal IT, i have decided to take the plunge and learn to become a full stack developer in 2020!

Whilst studying at Coder Academy, and two days a week at work, I will be leading a global team of experienced developers and creatives committed to creating simple, functional web portals for small firms in Australia, the United Kingdom and the United States.

My team consists of four developers (PHP, SQL, Javascript/NodeJS/jQuery/Vue.js, HTML, CSS, C#, JSON, REST), four quality engineers and one product manager (see my amazing team below).
